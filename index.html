About AS211622

Peering Policy

  • A public and routable ASN
  • Support IXP or Tunnel Peering
  • With one or more public routable IPv6 address space
  • Peering information must be available on PeeringDB
  • No SLA
  • Pleasure to peer for hobby purposes
